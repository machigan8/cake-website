About Roselly Cakes

Our Story

Welcome to Rossely Cakes, where passion meets perfection in every bite. Founded in 2020 in the vibrant city of Nairobi, we've been dedicated to creating memorable experiences through our handcrafted cakes.

Our journey began with a simple belief: every celebration deserves a perfect cake. From intimate birthdays to grand weddings, we pour our heart into every creation, using only the finest ingredients and time-honored techniques.

What sets us apart is our commitment to customization. We understand that every event is unique, and we work closely with our clients to bring their vision to life, creating not just cakes, but edible works of art.
